Ebdaa Outdoor

Artistic outdoor design solutions.

Our Company

Welcome to Ebdaa Outdoor - Technical Supervisors, an exterior design studio that specializes in creating beautiful and functional outdoor spaces. Our integrated consulting and supervision services provide design solutions for a wide range of outdoor areas, from swimming pools and fountains to landscaping, marble and stone pathways, natural and industrial wood, and modern lighting.

Our goal is to establish ourselves as a leading and distinguished consulting firm for outdoor design in Egypt and the entire region. We pride ourselves on our artistic vision and attention to detail, ensuring that each space we create is unique and reflective of our clients' personal style and needs.

Our Services

Swimming Pools

Ebdaa Outdoor - Technical Supervisors offers expert design and supervision services for swimming pools. Our experienced designers and architects work closely with clients to create stunning and functional swimming pools that meet their specific requirements. We use high-quality materials and modern techniques to ensure that each pool is not only beautiful but also long-lasting and easy to maintain. Whether you want a luxurious infinity pool or a family-friendly design, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

Coordination Of Civilization

Ebdaa Outdoor - Technical Supervisors designs and supervises waterfalls for outdoor spaces. We create visually stunning and functional waterfalls, using high-quality materials and modern techniques to ensure safety and ease of maintenance. Our customized designs cater to our client's preferences, with options for both natural and modern styles. Trust us to create a unique and personalized outdoor space that enhances the beauty and tranquility of your surroundings.
